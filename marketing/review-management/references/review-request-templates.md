# Review Request Templates

Ready-to-use templates for requesting reviews across all channels. Customize the [bracketed] fields for your business.

## In-Person Scripts

### Script 1 — Technician at Job Completion
> "Everything look good? Great! Hey, if you were happy with the work today, it would mean a lot if you could leave us a quick Google review. Takes about 30 seconds. I'll shoot you a text with the link right now."

### Script 2 — Office/Front Desk
> "Thanks for coming in today, [Name]. If you have a minute, we'd love a Google review — it really helps other folks in [City] find us. Here's a card with the QR code if you want to do it on your phone."

### Script 3 — After Positive Comment
> "That's really great to hear, thank you! If you wouldn't mind, putting that in a Google review would help us out more than you know. Can I text you the link?"

**Keys to success:**
- Ask while still face-to-face
- Send the link immediately (text or card)
- Keep it casual and genuine
- Don't pressure — one ask is enough

## SMS Templates

### Template 1 — Standard (Same Day)
> Hi [Name], thanks for choosing [Business]! If you have 30 seconds, a Google review would really help us out: [short URL] Thanks! - [Tech Name]

### Template 2 — After Specific Service
> Hi [Name], hope the [service type] is working perfectly! If you're happy with the work, we'd appreciate a quick review: [short URL] - [Business]

### Template 3 — Follow-Up (Day 2-3, if no response)
> Hi [Name], just checking in — everything still good after the [service]? If so, we'd love your feedback: [short URL] No pressure either way. Thanks!

**SMS rules:**
- Send within 2 hours of service completion (or next morning for evening jobs)
- One follow-up max — don't spam
- Include the direct Google review link (shortened)
- Personalize with customer name and/or tech name
- Keep under 160 characters when possible

## Email Templates

### Template 1 — Standard Post-Service

**Subject:** How did we do, [Name]?

> Hi [Name],
>
> Thank you for choosing [Business] for your [service type]. We hope everything went smoothly!
>
> If you have a minute, we'd really appreciate a quick Google review. Your feedback helps other homeowners in [City] find reliable [service type].
>
> **[Leave a Review →]** (button linked to Google review URL)
>
> Thanks again,
> [Your Name/Team]
> [Business Name]

### Template 2 — After Specific Service

**Subject:** Your [service type] — all good?

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Tech name] mentioned the [service type] at your [home/office] went well. Glad we could help!
>
> If you're happy with the work, a quick Google review would mean a lot to our team:
>
> **[Leave a Review →]**
>
> It only takes about 30 seconds and helps other folks in [City/Neighborhood] find quality [service type].
>
> Thanks,
> [Your Name]

### Template 3 — Relationship-Based (Insurance, Professional Services)

**Subject:** Quick favor, [Name]?

> Hi [Name],
>
> I hope you're settling in well with your new [policy type / service outcome]. It was great working with you on this.
>
> I have a small favor to ask — would you mind leaving us a brief Google review? A few sentences about your experience goes a long way in helping other [homeowners/families/businesses] in [City] find us.
>
> **[Leave a Review →]**
>
> Thank you for your trust in [Business Name]. We're always here if you need anything.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

**Email rules:**
- Send within 24 hours of service
- One clear CTA — don't ask for multiple platforms
- Keep it 3-5 sentences max (nobody reads long review requests)
- Personalize: use their name, mention specific service, mention tech
- Don't send to customers with unresolved issues

## QR Code Placements

Print QR codes linking directly to your Google review page and place on:

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Business cards | Back side, with "Leave a Review" text |
| Invoices | Bottom of page or email footer |
| Truck/van wraps | Near phone number, "Review us on Google" |
| Job site signs | "How did we do? Scan to review" |
| Receipts | Bottom of receipt, auto-printed |
| Waiting room | Framed sign with QR code |
| Email signature | Small icon + link or QR |
| Yard signs | "Scan for reviews" (doubles as social proof) |

## Automated Sequence (CRM Integration)

If you have a CRM (ServiceTitan, Jobber, HouseCall Pro, etc.):

**Trigger:** Job marked "Complete" in CRM

**Sequence:**
1. **Immediately:** SMS Template 1 (same-day text)
2. **24 hours later:** Email Template 1 (if no review detected)
3. **72 hours later:** SMS Template 3 (gentle follow-up, only if no review)
4. **Stop.** Three touches maximum. Never more.

**Platform routing:**
- 70% → Google review link
- 20% → Facebook recommendation
- 10% → Industry-specific (Angi, Clearsurance, etc.)
- Rotate platforms per customer to build presence across all

## What NOT to Do

- Never incentivize reviews with discounts or gifts (FTC violation)
- Never ask "Were you satisfied?" before sending the review link (review gating)
- Never ask specifically for a "5-star review" (against platform policies)
- Never send more than 3 touchpoints per customer
- Never solicit Yelp reviews directly (Yelp filters them aggressively)
- Never have staff write fake reviews
- Never use a third-party review generation service that creates fake profiles
