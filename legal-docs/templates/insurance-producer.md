## 2. Producer Obligations

Producer agrees to:
- Solicit business only for carriers and products authorized by Agency
- Follow all applicable state insurance laws, carrier guidelines, and Agency procedures
- Maintain accurate records of all solicitations and placements
- Act in the best interest of clients in making coverage recommendations
- Promptly report any errors, omissions, or complaints to Agency
- Maintain Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance with minimum limits of ${{EO_LIMITS}} per occurrence
- Not bind coverage without explicit authorization from Agency or the carrier

## 3. Agency Obligations

Agency agrees to:
- Appoint Producer with carriers listed in Schedule A (attached)
- Provide Producer access to carrier quotes, systems, and product information
- Process applications and forward commissions received from carriers within 15 days of receipt
- Provide reasonable training on products and compliance requirements

## 4. Compensation

**Commission splits:** Producer receives {{COMMISSION_PERCENTAGE}}% of the base commission received by Agency from the carrier on policies Producer writes.

**Renewals:** Producer receives {{RENEWAL_PERCENTAGE}}% on renewal commissions for policies in force, as long as Producer's agreement with Agency remains active and Producer services the account.

**Chargebacks:** If a policy is cancelled within {{CHARGEBACK_PERIOD}} days of inception and the carrier charges back the commission, the corresponding amount will be deducted from Producer's next commission payment. Producer is not liable for chargebacks due to carrier insolvency.

**Payment:** Commissions are paid within 15 days of Agency receiving payment from the carrier.

## 5. Ownership of Business

Business placed through Agency is owned by Agency. Upon termination, Producer has the right to move accounts to a new agency if: (a) the client requests the move in writing, and (b) Producer has not violated this agreement. Agency will not unreasonably withhold consent.

## 6. Errors and Omissions

Each party is responsible for its own E&O claims arising from its own acts or omissions. Agency is not responsible for E&O claims arising from Producer's recommendations, representations, or errors.

## 7. Confidentiality

Producer agrees to keep Agency's client lists, pricing, carrier relationships, and business methods strictly confidential. This obligation survives termination for 3 years.

## 8. Non-Solicitation

During this agreement and for **12 months** after termination, Producer will not solicit Agency employees or actively recruit Agency's clients to move to a competing agency.

## 9. Compliance

Both parties will comply with all applicable federal and state insurance laws, regulations, and carrier requirements. Producer will complete all continuing education requirements to maintain licensure.

## 10. Term and Termination

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated. Either party may terminate with **30 days'** written notice. Either party may terminate immediately for: license revocation, material breach, fraud, or misappropriation of funds.

On termination: Agency will pay all commissions earned through the termination date within 30 days. Producer will return all Agency materials and confidential information.
