The parties want to explore a potential business relationship and need to share confidential information with each other. This agreement protects both parties' confidential information.

## 1. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any information one party discloses to the other that is marked confidential, or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

Confidential Information does not include information that:
- Is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this agreement
- Was already known to the receiving party before disclosure
- Is independently developed by the receiving party without use of the confidential information
- Is received from a third party lawfully and without restriction

## 2. Obligations

Each party agrees to:
- Keep the other party's Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Use the Confidential Information only for the Purpose stated above
- Not disclose the Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent
- Protect the Confidential Information with at least the same care used to protect its own confidential information (and no less than reasonable care)
- Limit access to employees and contractors who need to know and are bound by similar confidentiality obligations

## 3. Required Disclosures

A party may disclose Confidential Information if required by law, court order, or government authority — but only after: (a) giving the other party prompt written notice (if legally permitted), (b) cooperating with the other party's efforts to obtain a protective order, and (c) disclosing only what is legally required.

## 4. Term

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **2 years**, unless earlier terminated by either party with 30 days' written notice. Obligations with respect to Confidential Information disclosed during the term survive for **3 years** after termination.

## 5. Return of Information

Upon request or at the end of this agreement, each party will promptly return or destroy (and certify destruction of) the other party's Confidential Information, except for copies retained in routine backup systems (which remain subject to confidentiality obligations).

## 6. No License

Nothing in this agreement grants either party any rights in the other party's Confidential Information except the limited right to use it for the Purpose. No intellectual property licenses are granted.

## 7. No Warranty

Confidential Information is provided "as is." Neither party makes any warranties about the accuracy, completeness, or fitness of any Confidential Information for any purpose.

## 8. Remedies

Each party acknowledges that breach of this agreement could cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate. Either party may seek injunctive relief (without bond) in addition to any other remedies.
