## 1. Purpose

Referrer agrees to refer prospective insurance clients to Agent/Agency. This agreement governs the terms of those referrals and any compensation.

## 2. Referral Process

When Referrer identifies a prospective client who may benefit from insurance products offered by Agent/Agency:
- Referrer will introduce the prospect with the prospect's consent
- Referrer will provide basic contact information and a brief description of the prospect's needs
- Referrer will not make coverage recommendations or quote premiums — that is Agent/Agency's role

## 3. Non-Licensed Activity

Referrer acknowledges that only licensed insurance producers may solicit, negotiate, or sell insurance. Referrer will not: quote coverages, explain policy terms, negotiate premiums, complete applications, or do anything requiring a producer license. Referrer's role is introduction only.

## 4. Compensation

**Referral fee:** {{REFERRAL_FEE}} ({{FEE_TYPE}}: flat fee per closed placement / percentage of first-year premium)

**Trigger:** Referral fee is earned when a referred prospect completes an application AND the policy is issued and paid in full.

**Payment:** Agent/Agency will pay referral fees within **30 days** of policy issuance.

**No fee on rejected/lapsed policies:** No referral fee is paid if the application is declined, the policy lapses within 60 days of inception, or the carrier charges back the premium.

**Compliance note:** Referral fees must comply with state insurance regulations. In states where producer-to-non-producer referral fees are restricted, compensation will be restructured to comply, or this provision will be void for that state only.

## 5. Client Data

Prospect information shared under this agreement is used only to facilitate the insurance placement. Agent/Agency will handle prospect data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

## 6. No Exclusivity

Nothing in this agreement prevents either party from referring to or working with other agencies or producers.

## 7. Term and Termination

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **1 year**, automatically renewing annually unless terminated with **30 days'** written notice. Termination does not affect fees earned on referrals made before the termination date.
