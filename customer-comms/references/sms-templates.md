# SMS Templates — Customer Communications

Copy-paste-ready SMS templates for local service businesses. All templates use standard `[placeholder]` tokens. Templates marked with (*) exceed 160 characters and will send as 2 SMS segments.

## Character Count Tips

- Business name uses ~10-20 characters. Factor this in.
- Phone numbers use 14 characters formatted as (555) 123-4567, or 10 unformatted.
- URLs should use a shortener if possible (bit.ly, your domain shortlink).
- Customer first names average 5-7 characters.
- If a template hits 165+ characters after substitution, consider shortening or splitting.

## Sending Best Practices

- **Transactional messages** (appointment, payment, job complete): Send anytime the event occurs.
- **Marketing messages** (seasonal, promos, review requests): 8am-7pm local time only.
- **Urgent messages** (weather, running late): Send immediately regardless of time.
- **Never send two SMS to the same customer within 4 hours** unless the second is a different transactional category (e.g., on-the-way followed by job complete is fine).
- **Always identify your business.** End with `- [Business]` to avoid looking like spam.
