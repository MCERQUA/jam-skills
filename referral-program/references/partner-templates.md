# Referral & Partnership Templates

Ready-to-use templates for trade partner agreements, real estate agent outreach,
property manager pitches, referral cards, email announcements, and social media
posts. Fill in the bracketed fields and deploy.

---

## 1. Trade Partner Referral Agreement

One-page, fill-in-the-blank agreement. Print two copies, both parties sign.
This is intentionally simple — a handshake with a paper trail.

---

**MUTUAL REFERRAL AGREEMENT**

**Date:** _______________

**Party A:**
- Business Name: ______________________________
- Contact Name: ______________________________
- Phone: ______________________________
- Email: ______________________________
- Trade/Service: ______________________________

**Party B:**
- Business Name: ______________________________
- Contact Name: ______________________________
- Phone: ______________________________
- Email: ______________________________
- Trade/Service: ______________________________

**Terms:**

1. **Purpose.** Party A and Party B agree to refer customers to each other when
   those customers need services offered by the other party. The goal is mutual
   business growth through trusted recommendations.

2. **Referral Process.** When a customer asks for a recommendation or could
   benefit from the other party's services:
   - Provide the other party's business name, phone number, and contact name.
   - Notify the other party of the referral (name + phone of the referred
     customer) within 24 hours via text or email.
   - The receiving party will contact the referred customer within 48 hours.

3. **Compensation.** (Select one)

   [ ] **Mutual referral — no fees.** Both parties refer freely. No money
       changes hands. Value is in the mutual lead flow.

   [ ] **Referral fee.** The receiving party pays the referring party $________
       for each referral that converts to a completed, paid job. Payment is due
       within 30 days of job completion.

   [ ] **Percentage fee.** The receiving party pays the referring party ________%
       of job revenue for each converted referral. Payment is due within 30
       days of job completion.

4. **Quality Standards.** Both parties agree to deliver quality workmanship and
   professional customer service to all referred customers. A referral reflects
   on the referring party's reputation.

5. **Exclusivity.** This agreement is non-exclusive. Both parties may maintain
   referral relationships with other businesses in the same trade.

6. **Tracking.** Both parties will maintain a log of referrals sent and received.
   Quarterly check-ins will be scheduled to review referral volume and address
   any issues.

7. **Duration.** This agreement is ongoing until either party terminates it with
   30 days written notice (text or email is sufficient).

8. **Disputes.** If either party is dissatisfied with the quality of service
   delivered to a referred customer, they will raise the issue directly with the
   other party before taking any other action.

**Signatures:**

Party A: ______________________________ Date: _______________

Party B: ______________________________ Date: _______________

## 2. Real Estate Agent Partnership Proposal

Send via email or print and deliver in person with your business card.

---

**Subject:** Partnership proposal — [Your Business Name] + [Agent Name/Brokerage]

Hi [Agent First Name],

I run [Your Business Name] — we're a [trade] company here in [City] with
[X] years of experience and [X]+ five-star reviews on Google.

I know real estate agents need trusted contractors they can count on for
pre-listing repairs, inspection items, and post-closing recommendations. I'd
like to be that contractor for your [trade] needs.

**Here's what I'm offering your clients:**

- **Priority scheduling.** Your clients go to the front of the line. Same-day
  or next-day estimates, guaranteed.
- **Fast turnaround.** I understand closings have deadlines. We work around your
  timeline, not ours.
- **Fair, transparent pricing.** Detailed written estimates before any work
  starts. No surprises.
- **Before/after photos.** Sent to you for every job so you can share with your
  clients and show the value.
- **Professional representation.** My team shows up on time, in uniform, and
  treats your clients' homes with respect. We make you look great.

**Here's what I'm offering you:**

- **Co-branded referral cards.** Business cards that say "Recommended by
  [Agent Name]" with your contact info alongside ours. Your clients see you
  as the resource connector.
- **Referral fee of $[X] per completed job** (or mutual referrals — I'm happy
  to recommend you when my customers mention buying or selling).
- **Direct line.** My personal cell: [Your Cell]. No phone trees, no callbacks.
  Text or call anytime.

I'm currently partnered with [X] other agents in [City] and the feedback has
been great — fast response, clean work, happy clients. I'd love to add you to
that list.

Can I buy you a coffee this week? I'm flexible on time and happy to come to
your office.

Best,
[Your Full Name]
[Your Title], [Business Name]
[Phone] | [Email] | [Website]

## 4. Referral Card Design Description

For printing — provide this description to your print shop or online printing
service (Vistaprint, GotPrint, etc.). Standard business card size: 3.5" x 2".

---

### Front of Card

**Layout:** Clean, professional. Business logo top-left or centered. High
contrast — dark text on light background or white text on branded dark
background.

**Content:**
```
[BUSINESS LOGO]

Love our service? Tell a friend!

YOU get $[X] off your next service
THEY get $[X] off their first visit

[Phone Number]
[Website URL]
```

**Design notes:**
- "YOU get / THEY get" should be the visual focal point — largest text after
  the logo
- Use brand colors for the reward amounts
- Phone number prominent — this is how most referrals will come in
- No clutter — white space is your friend
- Consider a simple icon (gift box, handshake, dollar sign) next to the rewards

### Back of Card

**Content:**
```
HOW IT WORKS

1. Give this card to a friend who needs [service type]
2. They call us and mention your name
3. You both save!

No limits. No expiration.
Refer as many friends as you like.

Referred by: ________________________________
             (friend writes your name here)

[Business Name] | [Phone] | [Website]
```

**Design notes:**
- Clean numbered list — easy to scan in 5 seconds
- "Referred by" line with a blank for the referrer's name — this is the
  tracking mechanism for walk-in/phone referrals
- "No limits. No expiration." builds confidence to hold onto the card
- Keep the back simpler than the front — one font, minimal design

### Print Specifications

- **Size:** 3.5" x 2" (standard business card)
- **Paper:** 16pt glossy or matte cardstock
- **Quantity:** Start with 500 (reorder in batches of 250-500)
- **Print:** Full color both sides
- **Estimated cost:** $25-40 for 500 cards at online printers

### Distribution Plan

- Every tech carries 20+ cards in their truck at all times
- Hand out 2-3 cards at every job completion (one for the customer, extras to
  give to friends)
- Leave 5-10 at trade partner offices
- Leave a stack at your own front desk/office
- Include one in every mailed invoice
- Pin one to the community boards at local hardware stores, coffee shops, gyms

## 5. Email Referral Program Announcement

Send to your full customer email list when launching the program.

---

**Subject:** You asked, we delivered — save $[X] every time you refer a friend

**Preview text:** Our new referral program rewards you AND your friends.

---

Hi [First Name],

We've got great news — we just launched our referral program, and it's
designed to thank YOU for spreading the word about [Business Name].

**Here's how it works:**

**You refer a friend** → You get **$[X] off** your next service
**They book with us** → They get **$[X] off** their first service

That's it. No forms, no codes, no hoops to jump through.

**How to refer:**

Just tell your friend to call us at **[Phone Number]** and mention your name.
Or forward this email — we'll take care of the rest.

There's no limit on referrals. Send us 1 friend or 10 — you save every single
time. And your rewards never expire.

**Why we're doing this:**

Honestly? Our best customers have always come from recommendations by people
like you. This is our way of saying thank you for the trust you've placed in
us — and making it worth your while to keep sharing.

Know someone who needs [service type]? Send them our way. You'll both be glad
you did.

Thanks for being part of the [Business Name] family.

[Owner First Name]
[Business Name]
[Phone] | [Website]

P.S. — Already know someone? Reply to this email with their name and number
and we'll reach out directly. You'll still get your $[X] reward when they book.

---

**Design notes for email:**
- Use a simple, clean layout — one column, no complex graphics
- Feature a visual "badge" or card graphic showing "Give $[X] / Get $[X]"
- CTA button: "Tell a Friend" (links to phone number or /referrals page)
- Mobile-optimized — 60%+ of your customers will read this on a phone
- Send on Tuesday or Wednesday at 10am for best open rates

## Usage Notes

- All templates use [bracketed placeholders] — fill these in with the business's
  actual information before sending or printing.
- Reward amounts ($[X]) should match what the business has set in their referral
  program configuration (see SKILL.md Section 2.2 for guidance on amounts).
- Trade partner agreement should be customized if the business has specific
  insurance or licensing requirements for partners.
- Email announcement should only be sent once — at program launch. Use the
  social media templates for ongoing promotion.
- All text can be adapted for the business's tone — more casual, more formal,
  more humor — as long as the core message (dual-sided reward, how to refer,
  no friction) remains clear.
